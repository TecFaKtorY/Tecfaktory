1. Investor Benefits Click to view

As a Tec Faktory investor, you will receive:

  • Regular ROI according to the chosen plan (profit-share, equity dividends, or hybrid).
  • Quarterly reports on revenue, expenses, and key performance metrics.
  • Flexibility to renew, convert, or exit as per the chosen plan's terms.
  • Strategic updates and invitations to key stakeholder meetings for equity investors.
  • Social impact — your investment supports local skills development and digital inclusion.
2. Investment Plans (Details) Click to view

Profit-Sharing Plan

Investors receive 10% of monthly net profit for a period of 12 months. Payments are made monthly after accounting and profit calculation. This plan does not confer ownership.

Equity Plan

Equity investors will receive an ownership stake of 10–15% depending on negotiation and investment size. Equity confers rights to dividends and strategic reporting. Equity is long-term and not subject to monthly payouts unless declared as dividends.

Hybrid Plan

This plan combines a smaller monthly profit share (5–7%) with an equity stake (5–10%). The hybrid plan offers immediate income with long-term upside.
